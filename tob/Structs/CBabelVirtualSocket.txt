====== CBabelVirtualSocket ======

0x40 bytes (CERTAIN)

* +16: Virtual socket number acquired with GetNewVirtualSocket
	* short virtualSocketNum
* +18: padding?
* +20: client pointer
	* [[CBabelClient]] * client
* +40: _RTL_CRITICAL_SECTION (16 bytes)

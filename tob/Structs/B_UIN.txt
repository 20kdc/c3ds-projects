====== B UIN ======

Identifies a specific user.
Represented by the form ''0+1'' where 0 is the UID and 1 is the HID.

===== Structure =====

'''
+0: UID
    int UID
+4: HID
    short HID
+6: 2 bytes of padding?
    short padding
'''
